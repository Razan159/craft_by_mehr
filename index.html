Our Story

Crafted From Heart

Hi, I'm the hands &
heart behind Pétale

What started as a gentle hobby — arranging wildflowers from my grandmother's garden — has blossomed into a lifelong devotion to handcrafting beauty that speaks louder than words.

Every bouquet I tie, every birthday gift I design, every anniversary box I curate carries a piece of my soul. I believe that handmade isn't just a method — it's a love language. When you gift something from Pétale, you're not just giving a product; you're giving a story, a feeling, a moment frozen in time.

Each creation is made with sustainably sourced materials, endless attention to detail, and the quiet hope that it will make someone's day a little more beautiful.

Mubeera PK.

Founder & Artisan
